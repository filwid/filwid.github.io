Information

Who are you?

My name is Filip Widepalm, I am 18 years of age and I live just outside of Gothenburg, Sweden. I currently attend Hulebäcksgymnasiet, an upper secondary school in my hometown and I would love to attend college in the future.

Why do you play golf?

I have been playing golf ever since I was nine years old when my dad got me into the game. I started taking lessons and fell in love with the game very fast; since then, I have been competing for several years and golf has now grown into a very big part of my life.

Why do you want to go to college?

I would love to go to college since I think it would be a very good opportunity for me to grow both as a golfer and as a person. It would also be a very good way to combine my desire to get an education with the pursuit of a career in the game of golf.

What are your strengths as a golfer?

I would say that my biggest strength is that I know my game very well. I know what risks I can and can not take on the golf course. Even if I have a bad day, I will still make the best of it and find a way to get through the round and put a respectable score together. I would also say that the shortgame is a strength of mine.
